# Troy-Heroldt-cs130-project

## CS 130 Final Project

### Summary:

An analysis of a coffee dataset, showing various kinds of coffee beans grown in different countries across the world. Giving insight into the country, kind of bean, altitude its grown, and different socre ratings. The dataset is unique in that it looks specifically at "speciality beans" which make up a large portion of the dataset (Beans that receive a total score of 80 or higher). 

In my data analysis I focus on three main relationships in the dataset. The average total coffee score of each country to determine the effects of geographical location. The affect of altitude on acidity and flavor scores. Finally, The relationship between aftertaste and flavor scores.
